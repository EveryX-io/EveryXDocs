# EVERYX GAMING

## EveryX Gaming

EveryX Gaming is our dedicated vertical for esports, competitive gaming, and streamer events. While our main platform covers everything from politics to economics, EveryX Gaming is laser-focused on the digital arena.

### What is EveryX Gaming?

We provide a specialized prediction market experience tailored for gamers.

* Esports Tournaments: Trade on outcomes for major tournaments (League of Legends, Dota 2, CS:GO, Valorant, and more).
* Streamer Events: Markets based on high-profile streamer challenges and showmatches.
* Real-time Odds: Dynamic probability adjustments based on live in-game events.

### Dedicated Gaming Communities

Because gaming culture moves fast, we have separate social channels dedicated entirely to this ecosystem. Follow these to stay ahead of the meta:

* Gaming X/Twitter: Live play-by-play updates, meme culture, and rapid market settlements.
* Gaming Facebook & Instagram: Highlights, clips, and community polls.
