# EVERYX SOCIALS

## Connect with EveryX

Welcome to the official EveryX community hub. Whether you are looking for real-time market updates, community discussions, or technical support, this is your starting point.

\{% hint style="warning" %\} Security Alert: Phishing and impersonation are common in the prediction market space. EveryX team members will NEVER DM you first to ask for your wallet keys, passwords, or funds. Always verify you are in the correct channel using the official links in the sidebar. \{% endhint %\}

### Official Channels

We maintain separate channels to ensure you get the information relevant to you:

#### News & Announcements

* EveryX X (Twitter): Follow for breaking news, new market listings, and platform updates.
* EveryX News Telegram: A read-only channel for critical alerts and daily summaries.

#### Community & Discussion

* Community Chat Telegram: The central hub for traders. Discuss market odds, share strategies, and chat with other users.
* Discord Server: Best for deeper discussions, organized topic threads, and developer updates.

#### Media & Content

* YouTube & TikTok: Watch tutorials, market analysis, and "How-To" guides to master the EveryX platform.
* Instagram & Facebook: Behind-the-scenes looks and community highlights.

### Community Guidelines

To keep our community safe and productive, please adhere to these rules:

1. Be Respectful: Healthy debate on market outcomes is encouraged; harassment is not.
2. No Shilling: Do not promote unrelated tokens or external projects.
3. Stay Safe: Report any suspicious DMs to a moderator immediately.
