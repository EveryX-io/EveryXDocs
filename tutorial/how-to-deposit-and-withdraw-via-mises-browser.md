# How to Deposit & Withdraw via Mises Browser

## How to Deposit & Withdraw via Mises Browser

Follow this guide to manage your funds on EveryX using the Mises mobile browser.

#### Prerequisites

* You must have the Mises Browser installed on your mobile device. (See our guide: [How to Set Up Mises Browser](https://www.google.com/search?q=%23)).
* You must have a small amount of POL (on Polygon Network) or BNB (on BNB Chain) in your wallet to pay for gas fees.

***

#### Part 1: How to Deposit

1\. Access EveryX Open your Mises browser and navigate to `everyx.io`.

2\. Navigate to History Locate and tap on the History tab in the menu.

3\. Initiate Deposit Tap the Deposit button.

4\. Transfer Funds Copy the wallet address provided on the screen. Go to your crypto wallet and send your funds directly to this address.

Note: The minimum deposit amount is $1.

***

#### Part 2: How to Withdraw

1\. Access Withdrawal Options Return to the History tab and tap on the Withdrawal button.

2\. Connect Wallet Ensure your wallet is connected to the site when prompted.

3\. Submit Withdrawal Follow the prompts to process your withdrawal.

Important Withdrawal Details:

* Minimum Withdrawal: $5
* Network Fee: $1
* Processing Time: Up to 24 hours

***
