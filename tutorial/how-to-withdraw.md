# How to Withdraw

## How to Withdraw Funds

Follow this guide to withdraw your earnings from EveryX directly to your connected wallet.

### Prerequisites

* Your account must be verified.
* You must have a minimum balance of $10 (the minimum withdrawal amount).
* Your MetaMask wallet must be connected.

***

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. Navigate to Portfolio Go to your main dashboard and click on the Portfolio tab.
2.  Access Withdrawal Options Click on Go to Deposit to open your transaction menu, then select the Withdrawal button.

    > _Note: You may be prompted to verify your account at this stage if you haven't done so already._
3. Enter Amount Input the amount you wish to withdraw. Ensure it meets the $10 minimum requirement.
4. Confirm Transaction Click the Withdraw button. Your request will be submitted for processing.
5. Receive Funds Once processed, the funds will be sent directly to your connected MetaMask wallet.

***

### Viewing Transaction History

To check the status of your withdrawal or view past activity:

* Click on View History.
* Here you can see a complete log of your transactions, including all deposits and withdrawals.
